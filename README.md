# FPCV-Notes

Concise notes and key points from the **First Principles of Computer Vision** lecture series by **Prof. Shree K. Nayar** (Columbia University) — structured for quick revision.

> 📺 Lecture Series: [YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@firstprinciplesofcomputerv3258)  
> 🌐 Official Website: [fpcv.cs.columbia.edu](https://fpcv.cs.columbia.edu)

---

## Why This Repo?

These notes are written while watching the FPCV lecture series, capturing key concepts, equations, and intuitions from each module. The goal is to have a single place for fast revision without re-watching the videos.

---

## Study Order

The notes are structured to follow the syllabus of a standard Computer Vision course (CSL7xx). Follow them in this order:

| # | File | Topics Covered 
|---|------|---------------
| 1 | [Image_Formation_Sensing_Binary.md](./Image_Formation_Sensing_Binary.md) | Pinhole camera, lenses, sensors, binary images 
| 2 | [Image_Processing.md](./Image_Processing.md) | Filtering, convolution, Fourier, deconvolution 
| 3 | [Edge_Detection_SIFT.md](./Edge_Detection_SIFT.md) | Edge detection, corner detection, SIFT descriptor 
| 4 | [Image_Stitching_Homography_RANSAC.md](./Image_Stitching_Homography_RANSAC.md) | Homography, warping, RANSAC, panoramas 
| 5 | [Camera_Calibration_Epipolar_Stereo.md](./Camera_Calibration_Epipolar_Stereo.md) | Camera model, calibration, epipolar geometry, stereo 
| 6 | [Optical_Flow_SfM_Object_Tracking.md](./Optical_Flow_SfM_Object_Tracking.md) | Optical flow, structure from motion, tracking 
| 7 | [Image_Segmentation.md](./Image_Segmentation.md) | k-means, mean-shift, graph-cut segmentation 
| 8 | [Neural_Networks.md](./Neural_Networks.md) | Perceptron, backpropagation, intro to deep learning
