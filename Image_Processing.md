Pixel Processing
Transformation T of intensity f at each pixel to intensity g:
Darken (f -128)
Lighten (f + 128)
Invert (255 - f)
Low Contrast(f/2)
High Contrast(f*2)
Gray( 0.3fr + 0.6fg + 0.1fb)
